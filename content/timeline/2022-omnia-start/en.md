---
title: "Started at Omnia"
summary: "My first education path in Finland: Finnish language, the local education system, and programming fundamentals."
---

I started at Omnia because it was my first education program in Finland. I needed to strengthen my Finnish, understand how the Finnish education system works, and build a practical foundation in software development.

For me, Omnia was not only a vocational school. It was a way to enter a new education environment step by step and test whether software development truly felt like the right professional direction.

During my studies, I also joined a preparatory course at Metropolia. Alongside my Omnia studies, I completed Metropolia assignments, which later helped me get accepted into higher education without an entrance exam.

This stage became an important bridge: from the decision to change profession to a real education path that later led me to Software Engineering studies at Metropolia University of Applied Sciences.
