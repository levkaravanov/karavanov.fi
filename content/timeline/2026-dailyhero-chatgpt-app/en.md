title: "DailyHero published in ChatGPT apps"
summary: "DailyHero was approved and published in ChatGPT apps, making it easier to connect the product to ChatGPT, including from mobile."
---

On July 7, 2026, DailyHero was approved and published in ChatGPT apps. For me, this is an important milestone: DailyHero is no longer only an iOS app and an MCP server, but also an app that can be opened directly inside ChatGPT.

The MCP server already made it possible to connect DailyHero to AI tools, but for a regular user that still meant extra setup. Publishing the app in ChatGPT makes the connection much simpler, including from a phone.

Functionally, this is still a more limited version of DailyHero than the main app. Architecturally, though, it is a separate interface to the same product: it works on top of the larger DailyHero codebase and shows how family tasks, rewards, and app data can gradually move into a conversational interface.
