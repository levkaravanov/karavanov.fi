Paid freelance work on a commercial project under NDA. The product details, client, and business logic are not public, but this stage matters for the portfolio: it was real client work with delivery responsibility.

The task was to build an MVP from scratch. I was responsible for the frontend and design: based on the specification, I created the full Figma design, worked through the interface architecture, user flow, screen states, navigation, and the main product flow.

After the design stage, I implemented the client side of the app in Flutter. The project targeted three platforms from the start: iOS, Android, and Web.

The stack included Flutter, Firebase, and GraphQL. Development was done in Cursor and Codex with an active AI-assisted workflow.
